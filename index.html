About Me...

Every person has their own unique story. Here is a glimpse into mine. I have been in the customer service industry for almost a decade. However, in 2021, just like everyone else in the US, I have decided to make a significant career change. I enrolled myself in Rutgers Coding Bootcamp to start my new career as a Web Developer, and I am incredibly excited.
I live in Rahway, New Jersey, with my daughter and a fluffy pet. My daughter is my greatest motivation and the reason I am doing this transition.
Outside of work and parenting, I enjoy running, hiking, biking, and any type of workout.
A fun fact about me, I was born in Colombia and raised on a multi-generational coffee plantation. I can say I come from where the best coffee in the world is grown. The fun fact is that, ironically, my family only drinks instant coffee, and I never got to taste the best coffee in the world until much later in life.
